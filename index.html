The world this week

Politics

文章日期: July 16th 2026

01美国与伊朗之间的停火几乎宣告破裂。此前，唐纳德·特朗普恢复了对霍尔木兹海峡伊朗船只的封锁，并下令发动新一轮打击，以削弱伊朗攻击商业航运的能力。伊朗继续把该地区国家作为目标，包括科威特和约旦，并用巡航导弹击中了两艘阿拉伯联合酋长国的油轮。美国袭击了一艘驶向哈尔克岛的油轮，哈尔克岛是伊朗的石油出口枢纽。特朗普曾提出美国可能对通过该海峡的船只收取20%的费用，但随后很快退让。油价大涨。

02Iraq’s new prime minister, Ali al-Zaidi, visited the White House. Mr Trump was effusive in his praise, describing Mr al-Zaidi as a “great leader”. The president had in effect blocked the return of Nouri al-Maliki as prime minister, viewing him as too close to Iran. Iraq’s new leader is under intense pressure to disarm Iranian-aligned militias that have launched attacks on American facilities.

03以色列大选将于10月27日举行。当以色列议会于7月17日解散时，贝尼亚明·内塔尼亚胡政府将已完成一个完整任期。

04Oxford University launched the first trial of a vaccine against the strain of Ebola that has broken out in the Democratic Republic of Congo. Nearly 800 people have died and over 2,000 are infected. Scientists think that the current outbreak could be worse than the one that killed more than 11,000 people in west Africa ten years ago, unless there is an improvement in efforts to combat the disease.

05来自南卡罗来纳州的美国资深参议员林赛·格雷厄姆意外去世，享年71岁。他刚从乌克兰返回，并正推动一项扩大对俄罗斯制裁的法案。特朗普称格雷厄姆是“伟大的政治家”，尽管这位参议员曾反对特朗普首次竞选总统，只是在特朗普胜选后才成为坚定盟友。格雷厄姆还支持以色列的战争目标，并主张对伊朗采取强硬立场。

06A suspect was arrested in the murder of Ann Widdecombe, a former MP and Conservative government minister in Britain who switched to the populist-right Reform UK and was its justice spokeswoman. Ms Widdecombe, a devout Christian who converted to Catholicism and fiercely resisted the transgender movement, was found dead at her home. Reform called for better security protection for MPs after a man was arrested for threatening on social media to shoot its leader, Nigel Farage.

07安迪·伯纳姆轻松获得足够多工党议员的支持，成为该党新领袖，这确保了他将接替基尔·斯塔默爵士。斯塔默爵士在下议院最后一次接受首相质询。“我为我们取得的一切成就感到自豪，”这位民意满意度为英国首相有记录以来最低的人说道。

08El Salvador’s president, Nayib Bukele, was nominated by his New Ideas party to run for a third term at next year’s election. When Mr Bukele was first elected in 2019 presidents were limited to one term. But in 2021 the country’s top court ruled that he could run a second time. Last year the legislative assembly passed a constitutional amendment scrapping term limits for the presidency altogether and extending the term in office from five years to six. Some observers fear that El Salvador is slipping back to the old Latin American model of one-man rule.

09委内瑞拉近期两次地震造成的死亡人数接近5,000人。政府正与国际货币基金组织讨论动用其在该基金的特别提款权，用于人道主义用途。

10At least 32 people died when a fire engulfed a bar in Bangkok. Survivors described how they encountered locked doors and poor signage when they tried to escape the inferno.

11在巴基斯坦管辖的克什米尔地区，围绕地方议会中为居住在巴基斯坦其他地区的难民保留的席位，又爆发了更严重的暴力冲突。一个草根组织“联合行动委员会”因煽动抗议已被取缔；该组织声称，保留席位削弱了当地代表性。最近的动乱造成9人死亡。过去几周约有30人死亡。

12Almost 2m people were evacuated in China from the path of Typhoon Bavi, a gigantic storm that spanned 1,000km (620 miles) at its widest point. Bavi made landfall at Taizhou, around 300km south of Shanghai, before turning on Wenzhou, which is a little farther south.

13沃洛德米尔·泽连斯基重组内阁，导致尤利娅·斯维里登科在担任总理仅一年后下台。“乌克兰正在改变其政治战略，”泽连斯基说。国防部长米哈伊洛·费多罗夫也被免职；他因乌克兰成功运用新型战时技术而广受赞誉，但此前与将领们发生冲突。与此同时，俄罗斯又对基辅发动了一轮猛烈轰炸，这是本月第五次。

14Lithuania’s ruling coalition approved Mindaugas Sinkevicius as the new prime minister. The coalition in parliament voted for his manifesto, which includes keeping spending on defence to at least 5% of GDP and an aim to keep American troops in the country for the long term. Although Russia is suffering heavy losses in Ukraine, it would be a mistake to think its “military threat is subsiding”, said Mr Sinkevicius.

15匈牙利议会通过了一项宪法修正案。该议会在4月选举后由中间派掌握超级多数。这项修正案将把陶马什·舒约克赶下总统职位，并解除彼得·波尔特宪法法院院长职务。新任总理彼得·马扎尔表示，两人都把对前右翼总理维克托·欧尔班的忠诚置于匈牙利利益之上。

16Border controls between Gibraltar, a British overseas territory, and Spain were abolished. Checks had been administered on people crossing to and from Spain, and thus the European Union, but during the Brexit negotiations with Britain the EU insisted on allowing free movement. Spaniards and Gibraltarians can now cross their border with ease. But as Gibraltar is now in effect part of the EU’s Schengen free-movement area, non-Gibraltarians will have to endure the EU’s new and technically challenged electronic border system at the airport.
